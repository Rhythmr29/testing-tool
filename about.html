About LeakScan

Welcome to LeakScan! We are dedicated to helping individuals and organizations ensure their data remains secure. Our platform allows users to check if their sensitive information, such as emails, usernames, or mobile numbers, has been exposed in data breaches.

We prioritize transparency and security by guaranteeing that we never store user inputs. Our mission is to create a safer digital world by empowering everyone with the tools they need to protect themselves online.
